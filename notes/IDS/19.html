Versioning Slides

  • Version 1 is very special. It defines what the extensibility will be.
  • Everything from that point on should match version 1.

Compatitbility

  • Full vs Acceptable compatibility
  • Partial Understanding

Postel's Law

  • "Be liberal in what you accept and conservative in what you send."
  • Originally meant to stop systems from being brittle.

Controversy

  • Browsers started being too liberal in what they expect.
  • Servers would transmit jpegs and call them plain text.
  • Browsers started just sniffing at the stream, and displaying data as images if they just looked like images.
