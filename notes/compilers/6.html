Parsing

  • Derivation: Take grammar, "prove" string is in language.
    • expr(expr(expr(var(x)) op(-) expr(y)) op() expr(int(2)))
    • expr(epxr(var(x)) op(-) expr(expr(var(y)) op() expr(int(2))))
  • Sentential Form: intermediary string, consisting of terminals and nonterminals.
  • Can work from leftmost (top down) or rightmost (bottom up) nonterminal.
  • Top Down parsers = LL, Bottom Up parsers = LR.
  • Want to have an online algorithm - pick up one token and use it.
