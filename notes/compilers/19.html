Register Allocation

  • Constraints:
    • Number of registers (K)
    • Liveness
    • Instructions
    • Special purpose registers
  • Goals:
    • Frequently used variables get registers
      • Nesting of loops (multiply by constant)
    • Reduce spill cost.
      • Measured by heuristic, profiling
