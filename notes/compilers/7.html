More on Grammar and Parsers

Top-Down Parsers

  • Recursive descent: use lookaheads.
    • Lookaheads: just tells us what's coming nexts.
    • Multiple lookaheads can be used to disambiguate productions.
    • Typically don't allow arbitrarily many lookaheads.
    • Grammar should be factored to allow this.
  • Parser combinators: do both things. Cost could be really big.

Bottom-Up Parsers

  • Assemble terminal symbols into trees, building tree up.
  • Reduction (reverse of derivation): Take string of tokens, replace with higher nonterminals.
  • Perform reductions left to right in order to get rightmost derivation.
